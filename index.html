Welcome to the Capoeira Songbook.

Music is a fundamental part of capoeira and how we understand and connect with the culture and history behind it.

At its core, this is a collection of songs sung in our group all in one space for easy reference.

However...

The Capoeira Songbook is an ever growing project and we intend to add new sections to delve into the hidden meanings, stories, and histories behind the music.
